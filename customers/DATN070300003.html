About Us

Learn More About Us

Day ATM is a revolutionary division of Nadan Payment Services Private Limited (NPays). It is a business entity that has ventured into the field of Payment Services and forayed into the deployment of White Label ATM Machines (WLAs), Cash@POS and Android Based mPOS - mini ATM cum card swiping machines distribution.

Vision

To make use of the untapped potentials of our banking system to develop a one-stop solution by digitally uniting the banks to make sure we grow together.

Mission

To create a micro-level networked infrastructure for the deployment of POS@Automatic Teller Machines, Day ATM KIOSKS, and mPOS devices to all the quarters of our country

Our Products

We do offer wide range of Services

Our Services are designed to be user-friendly and profitable.

Cash Withdrawal

We support Cash Withdrawal from our ATM Machine through All Banks (recognized under the Reserve Bank of India) - Debit Cards, based on the same transaction limits offered by the Parent Bank.

Cash Deposit

We support Cash Deposit from our ATM Machine to All Banks (recognized under the Reserve Bank of India), based on the same transaction limits offered by the Parent Bank.

Bharat Bill Payment System (BBPS)

We are the pioneers in integrating Bharat Bill Payment System within an ATM Machine. We offer all the Bill Payment Services like Mobile Recharge, DTH, Electricty Bills and what not.

AEPS

We are one among very few ATM Services which offer Aadhar Enabled Payment System. Our AEPS system is encrypted, and is designed to be highly user-friendly.

`

Biometric Payment System

We are a unique ATM Service to offer Biometric Payment System. Our BPS system is also encrypted, and is designed to be highly user-friendly.

Direct Money Transfer (DMT)

Last but not the least, Our ATM Machines do support Direct Money Transfer (DMT), and there is no match for our quality of DMT services.
